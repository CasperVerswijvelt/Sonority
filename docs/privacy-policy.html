Sonority — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Sonority is a utility for configuring compatible speakers on your local Wi-Fi network. This policy explains, plainly, what the app does and does not do with your information. The short version: it collects nothing.

Information we collect

None. Sonority has no user accounts and no sign-in. It does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. It contains no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

How the app works

Sonority communicates only with speakers on the same local Wi-Fi network as your device, using their standard local network interface. To do this, the operating system asks your permission for Local Network access. This communication stays on your network — no data about your speakers, your configuration, or your usage is sent to us or to any third party. The app has no backend server.

On-device storage

The app may keep small preferences (such as remembered settings) on your device using the operating system's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Children's privacy

Sonority is not directed at children and collects no information from anyone.

Changes to this policy
