Sonority — logo concept archive

The brainstorm that produced the current mark. Chosen: 9c-3 — three speaker cabinets (tweeter + woofer), a white front speaker flanked by two grey ones whose tops peek a little higher. Kept here so the alternatives are easy to revisit.

Icon vs. lockup decision: the app icon is mark-only and large on every platform (Apple HIG discourages app-name text in icons; Android masks clip a wordmark — and the OS already prints the name under the icon). The SONORITY wordmark lives in the full logo lockup (splash + marketing + logo.html), where the mark stays big. Icon mark scale s = 1.15.
